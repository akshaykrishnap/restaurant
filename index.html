About us

Day 2 Day is a 50 year old Indian restaurant that specializes in authentic and delicious cuisine from different regions of India. The restaurant was founded by Mr. and Mrs. Singh, who immigrated to the UK in the 1970s and wanted to share their passion for food with the local community. Day 2 Day offers a variety of dishes, from spicy curries and tandoori meats to vegetarian options and desserts. The restaurant also caters for special occasions and events, such as weddings, birthdays and corporate functions. Day 2 Day is known for its friendly service, cozy atmosphere and affordable prices. The restaurant has won several awards and accolades over the years, and has a loyal customer base that keeps coming back for more. Day 2 Day is more than just a restaurant, it is a family tradition that celebrates the rich and diverse culture of India.
